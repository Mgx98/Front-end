Welcome to Maashaven

Description

The station was constructed on February 9, 1968. The station is very high and has a fairly impressive structure. The station hall "floats" above the street level, directly below the platforms. Exits connect the hall directly with the tram stop. Two lifts, which were not part of the original design of the station and were installed later, lead directly from the platforms to the street.

Transport

The Rotterdam metro station Maashaven is a station of line D and line E located on a viaduct at the end of the Maashaven on the south bank of the Nieuwe Maas. You can change to tram line 2 at Maashaven station.
